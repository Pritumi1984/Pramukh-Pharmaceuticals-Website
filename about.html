Who Are we?

Established as a sole trader company in 2006 by Mr. Tushar Patel, Pramukh Pharmaceuticals Ltd is focused in the retail and wholesale distribution of healthcare goods such as medicines and health care equipment. We are located right in the center of Kampala City hence we are accessible to everyone in the country.

What do we do?

For more than 14 years, Pramukh pharmacy is sincerely dedicated to provide the highest customer service standards and quality healthcare in the region. We distribute and sell medicines and a variety of healthcare equipment with a commitment towards customer satisfaction and healthcare socio-economic growth.



Our vision

Our vision is to be the leading provider of healthcare goods and services in the country.

Our mission

Our mission is to establish a first-class retail pharmacy with a wide range of quality generic and branded medical drugs at the most affordable prices.
